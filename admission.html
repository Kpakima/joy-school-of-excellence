Frequently asked questions

01

When does admission begin?

Admission typically open several months before the start of each academic year. Please visit our news section or contact the admissions office for current dates

02

Is there an entrance examination?

Some grade levels my require an entrance Assessment or interview to help us understand each student's academic readiness

03

Can students transfer from another school?

Yes, Transfer students are welcome, subject to available space and submission of the required academic records and supporting documents

04

What are your school hours?

School hours vary slightly by grade level. Detailed schedules are provided during registration and orientation.

05

How do I Know if my child's application has been accepted?

Families will recieve an official admission notification by email or telephone after the application review process has been completed

06

Can I visit the school before applying?

Absolutely! We encourage parents and prospective students to schedule a campus visit to meet our staff, explore our facilities, and learn more about our programs
